title: "Loss functions measure the agent, not just the project"
status: "active"
date: "2026-04-01"
---

Traditional metrics measure software (test coverage, bug count). Loss-forge metrics also measure the AI agent operating the software: tokens consumed per session, time to complete a task, context needed to make a change, corrections needed after a change. The agent is part of the system. Its efficiency is a first-class metric.
